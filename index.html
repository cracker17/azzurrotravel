Our Story

More Than Booking Travel —
Building Relationships

Azzurro Travel was founded in 2008 by Marjory Werstuk in Marina Del Rey, California, with a vision that ran counter to the direction of the travel industry: in an era of online booking engines and automated itineraries, she believed the human touch would always be the most valuable thing a travel agency could offer.

Over 17 years later, Azzurro Travel has grown into a premium concierge agency trusted by some of Hollywood's most prominent producers, A-list talent, Fortune 500 executives, and discerning leisure travelers. We've booked travel across every continent, navigated every complexity, and delivered on every promise.

The name Azzurro — Italian for the deep blue of a clear sky — reflects both our Southern California roots and our global aspirations. It's the color of the ocean horizon: of endless possibility, of the world waiting to be explored.

"We believe that booking travel is most productive and pleasurable through one-on-one specialized concierge relationships. Our exceptional personalized service is the most important product we supply."
