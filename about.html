Our Story

More Than Booking Travel —
Building Relationships

Azzurro Travel was founded in 2008 by Marjory Werstuk in Marina Del Rey, California, with a vision that ran counter to the direction of the travel industry: in an era of online booking engines and automated itineraries, she believed the human touch would always be the most valuable thing a travel agency could offer.

Over 17 years later, Azzurro Travel has grown into a premium concierge agency trusted by some of Hollywood's most prominent producers, A-list talent, Fortune 500 executives, and discerning leisure travelers. We've booked travel across every continent, navigated every complexity, and delivered on every promise.

The name Azzurro — Italian for the deep blue of a clear sky — reflects both our Southern California roots and our global aspirations. It's the color of the ocean horizon: of endless possibility, of the world waiting to be explored.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

People First

Every traveler is a person with a story, not a booking number. We invest in understanding who you are before we open a reservation system.

Accountability

When we say we'll do something, it gets done. When something goes wrong — we own it and fix it. No excuses, no finger-pointing.

Transparency

We believe in honest communication — about pricing, options, and trade-offs. We tell you what you need to know, not just what you want to hear.

Curiosity

The world is vast and full of extraordinary places most people never discover. We stay curious and bring that knowledge to every client interaction.

Excellence

Good enough never is. Whether booking a $400 shuttle or a $40,000 charter, we approach every reservation with the same standard of excellence.

Passion

We genuinely love travel and what we do. That passion isn't a marketing line — it's the reason our team members have been in this industry for decades.
